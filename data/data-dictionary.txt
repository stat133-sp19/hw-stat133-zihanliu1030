team_name: The name of the players' team.\
game_date: Indicates the date of the game.\
season: Indicates which season the game is at.\
period: Indicates which 12-minute period the game is at.\
minutes_remaining, seconds_remaining: The amount of time in minutes and seconds, respectively, that remained to be played in a given period.\
shot_made_flag: Indicates whether a shot was made (y) or missed (n).\
action_type: Indicates the basketball moves used by players.\
shot_type: Indicates whether a shot is a 2-point field goal, or a 3-point field goal.\
shot_distance: Distance to the basket (measured in feet).\
opponent: The name of the opponents' team.\
x, y: the court coordinates (measured in inches) where a shot occurred .\
